Frequently Asked Questions

  • MedYouth is open to all high school and university students across Cyprus who have a passion for medicine, healthcare, and the sciences. You don't have to be a medical student to join we welcome anyone with a curious mind!

    Who is eligible to join MedYouth?

  • As a member, you'll get exclusive access to our events, including practical workshops (like first aid and suturing), talks from leading medical professionals, and unique volunteering opportunities in healthcare settings. It's a fantastic way to gain experience, build your network, and connect with like-minded peers.

    What are the benefits of becoming a member?

  • Not at all! MedYouth is for anyone interested in the broader field of healthcare. Whether your passion lies in becoming a doctor, nurse, biomedical researcher, pharmacist, or public health official, our events are designed to provide valuable insights for all future healthcare leaders.

    Do I have to be a pre-med student to join?

  • Yes! For most of our workshops, seminars, and major events, we provide a certificate of participation. These are great for including in your CV and university applications to showcase your commitment to the medical field.

    Do members receive certificates for participation?

  • Getting involved is easy! You can sign up through the membership form on our website or by contacting us on our social media channels. Follow us for updates on our next event we'd love to see you there!
