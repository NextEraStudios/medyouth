What is MedYouth?

The No.1 Club in Cyprus for:
Aspiring Doctors & Med Enthusiasts

It’s not your typical lecture — it’s an inspiring experience built for future medical leaders. Join us for talks, workshops, and real hospital insights right here in Cyprus — where science meets passion and community.
